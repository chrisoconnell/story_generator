Back at CyberShield, the team cheered as the AI came back online. Traffic lights turned green, water mains shut off, and the mayor called Alex personally to thank him. “You saved Neonspire!” she exclaimed. Alex just grinned, adjusting his “Iron Man” T-shirt—the same one he’d worn in the warehouse, now a little dusty. “All in a day’s work,” he said, heading back to his cubicle for another energy drink.

From that day on, Alex was known as the “Code Hero” of Neonspire, the programmer who turned a city’s darkest hour into a legendary tale of tech triumph. And somewhere in his cluttered cubicle, CodeSnipe waited for its next adventure.
